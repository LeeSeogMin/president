## Key Observations

1. **Korea surpassed OECD average in 2019**: The gap turned positive for the first time, reaching +0.1977.
2. **Steady improvement under Moon Jae-in**: Korea's GE.EST rose from 1.03 (2017) to 1.37 (2021), while the OECD average declined.
3. **OECD average has been declining**: From a peak of ~1.30 (2004) to ~1.07 (2023), reflecting governance challenges across member countries.
4. **Korea's peak**: 1.4048 in 2023, the highest value in the entire series.
5. **Park Geun-hye period reversal**: After gains under Lee Myung-bak, Korea's score declined during the Park administration (2013-2016).
