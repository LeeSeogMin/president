## Task 3-1: Ostrom IAD Framework - Theoretical Foundation

### Rules-in-Form vs. Rules-in-Use (Ostrom, 2005)

**Core Distinction**:
- **Rules-in-form**: Formal legal rules, statutes, regulations as written in official documents
- **Rules-in-use**: Rules that participants actually refer to when explaining and justifying their actions; the operational norms governing real behavior in action situations

Ostrom emphasized that formal regulations (rules-in-form) do not always match the rules that actors actually follow in practice (rules-in-use). The gap between these two reveals institutional effectiveness or dysfunction.

**Operationalization Methodology**:
1. **Institutional Grammar Tool (IGT)** (Crawford & Ostrom, 1995): Decomposes institutional statements into ADICO components (Attribute, Deontic, aIm, Condition, Or else)
2. **Seven Rule Types** (meso-level): position, boundary, choice, aggregation, information, payoff, and scope rules
3. **Measurement approach**: Semi-structured interviews with key informants + analysis of formal regulations -> compare formal text vs. actual practice through business flow analysis
4. **Key reference**: Carter et al. (2015) "Assessing Policy Divergence: How to Investigate the Differences between Law and a Corresponding Regulation"

**Relevance to Institutional Adaptive Capacity**:
- The rules-in-form vs. rules-in-use gap is a direct measure of institutional adaptive capacity
- Systems with large gaps indicate formal institutions that fail to adapt to real-world conditions
- The State-reinforced self-governance (SRSG) framework builds on Ostrom to introduce design principles and a typology of institutional capacities for assessing adaptive/transformative capacity
- For our INST dimensions: each dimension can be assessed by comparing formal legal mandates (rules-in-form) with actual implementation data (rules-in-use)

### D1: 정책 피드백 (Policy Feedback) - 정부업무평가

#### Legal Framework (Rules-in-Form)
- **정부업무평가 기본법** (2006년 제정, 시행)
- 국무총리가 정부업무평가위원회 심의/의결을 거쳐 기본계획 수립
- 매년 3월말까지 연도별 시행계획 수립
- 평가유형: **자체평가** (중앙행정기관 자율) + **특정평가** (국무총리 주관, 2개 이상 부처 관련 시책)
- **법적 의무**: 기재부장관은 평가결과를 다음 연도 예산편성에 반영해야 함
- 결과공개: 확정일(정평위 의결)로부터 1개월 이내 국회 상임위 및 부처 홈페이지 공개

#### Operational Reality (Rules-in-Use)
- 결과 공개 및 국회보고 등 **형식적/법절차적 측면에서는 잘 운영**
- 그러나 **인사, 예산편성, 조직개편, 인력운영에의 실질 반영은 미흡** (학술연구 결과)
- 2017년 기준: 평가대상사업 총 예산의 약 **1% 수준**만 구조조정
- 환류 효용성이 낮다는 학술적 비판 다수 존재
